Use Thmanyah (found in project)

Premium Arabic typeface — elegant, editorial, used by Thmanyah Media and top MENA brands. More distinctive, higher perceived quality for Arabic readers. Requires extracting and self-hosting the font.

Use IBM Plex Sans Arabic (spec default)

Clean, neutral, highly legible — used by many tech platforms. Loads via Google Fonts CDN, zero setup. Great for UI text and body copy. Excellent accessibility.

C

Use Thmanyah for headings, IBM Plex for body

Best of both: Thmanyah's editorial personality for big display text, IBM Plex's readability for body copy and UI. Mirrors the Fraunces + Manrope pairing used in English.
